The Simpsons Hit & Run - Cheats Guide

Complete Cheats List

PS2 - Top Left Xbox - Top Right
Gamecube - Bottom Left PC -Bottom Right

Vehicle Invincibility

When you enter any vehicle with this cheat applied, the vehicle takes NO damage.

More Camera Angles

A free camera to explore the map.

Horn Jump

Pressing the horn button will make the car jump high into the air

Redbrick Car

All bonus vehicles are replaced with the Red Brick car model.

Vehicle One Hit Kill

Any traffic you collide with explodes after a single hit.

Grid View

Reveals the debug collision map to display level geometry and boundaries as a wireframe grid.

Display Speedometer

A speedometer appears above the mini-map to display current speed.

Unlock All Vehicles

Unlocks every vehicle from the phonebooth menu. requires a 100% completed save file.

Change Player Skin

Kicking with this cheat enabled cycles through the active character model.Requires a 100% completed save file.

Faster Acceleration

The top speed limit is removed from all vehicles.

Even Faster Acceleration!

The acceleration for every vehicle is increased significantly.

Psychedelic View

Alters the view to become distorted and wavey, simulating the effects of being intoxicated!

How to Access and Enable Cheats

Within the main menu, go to the settings option and hold down the button for the respective system

L1 and R1 for Playstation 2

L and R for Xbox and Gamecube

F1 for PC

While holding down the button, imput the cheat code and a sound will play if done correctly.
